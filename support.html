Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work without an internet connection?

Yes. İkra: Niyet is designed to work entirely offline. The Qurʾān text, translations, and the default recitations are available without a network connection.

Where are my bookmarks and settings stored?

All of your data — bookmarks, settings, and downloaded recitations — is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

How do I download additional recitations?

Additional reciters can be downloaded from within the app. Once downloaded, they are stored on your device and play back offline.

Do you collect any personal data?

No. The app does not create accounts, track activity, or use analytics or advertising. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Reporting an issue

When reporting a problem, it helps to include your device model, OS version, and a short description of what happened so we can resolve it faster.
